  • Bonafide Certificate

  • Bonafide Certificate is a document which is issued as a proof that an individual belongs to the particular education institute. It is a certificate of proof that he/ she is a student of a particular class and course in the institution during a specified time period.
  • In case, if you don't have bonafide certificate, you can take it from Admission Department during office hours.


  • Admission Acknowledgement

  • An admission letter is used by institute to tell a prospective student that he or she has passed the admissions of the academic institution. More so, an admission letter may also be intended for confirming that a person have been admitted to an entity, event, or any other circumstance.
  • Admission Acknowledgement is provided to the students after admission process.
